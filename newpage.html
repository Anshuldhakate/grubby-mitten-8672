WOLDSEND HOLIDAY COTTAGES

Beautifully Located Between the North York Moors & Yorkshire Wolds

❮ ❯

Finished to a very high standard, Granary & Rose Cottages retain many of the original features of the original 18th Century Barn. Original roof trusses, a brick dovecote and original pantiles, all helping to highlight the character of this wonderful homely building. The cottages were renovated and finished in 2010. Since then we have had many satisfied people stay with us.

We are in Rillington, which is a small, unspoilt village just 5 miles from Malton and 20 miles from York in a quiet valley lying between the North Yorkshire Moors and the undiscovered rolling hills of the Yorkshire Wolds. The village comprises of country cottages and working farms, there is a Post Office and General Store, Fish & Chip Shop, our Local Butcher, a church, The Coach & Horses and The Fleece public houses are all within a 5 minute walk from the cottages.

The cottages were converted by award winning Kent Building Developments Ltd and have a number of energy saving features built in. They are heated by a high percentage of renewable energy, air source heat pumps provide all the energy necessary to run the highly efficient underfloor heating system and all of the heat required for the hot water system.
